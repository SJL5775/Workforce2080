About the Creator

Stephen James Leitz, MBA, PHR, SHRM-CP, FPC

Founder and CEO of Workforce 2080

Stephen Leitz is a certified HR professional with over 6 years experience in the field ranging from the Recruitment experience all the way to terminations. Stephen also has almost 3 years of Human Capital Management Software Experience at Netchex. Stephen thoroughly enjoys listening to music and discovering any new music from the 80's and 90's which he has heard before or a long time ago. Stephen created Workforce 2080 as a means to give employees the ability to calculate their net paychecks as well as get legal help for their garnishments from local attorney's in their region.

Stephen hopes this website enables employees to be empowered with more information and gives them piece of mind with their final wages after taxes and deductions. Stephen also has a passion about finding work for those who are looking for jobs.
